Introduction

Welcome to the introduction design project, focused on gamifying a learning app. Our aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users

Gamification involves applying game design principles to non-game context, such as education. The objective is to increase user motivation, engagement and enjoyment.

This project involves various phases, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and usabilty testing. The competitive analysis will help me identify strengths and weaknesses in the exsisting market, and highlight on areas we can improve.

Wireframing and prototyping allowed us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable learning process.

Finally, usability testing was conducted to gather feedback from users on the app's usability and functionality. The feedback will be used to refine and improve the app, ensuring that the user's needs are met.

Overall, our aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyable, ultimately improving the user's learning outcomes.
